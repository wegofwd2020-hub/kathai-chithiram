# Your privacy — in plain words

**Version:** 2026-07-01 · For parents and guardians using Kathai Chithiram

> This is the plain-language version of our privacy commitment. The full internal
> policy is in `PRIVACY.md`; if the two ever disagree, `PRIVACY.md` is the source
> of truth. Nothing here is legal advice.

Kathai Chithiram turns a short story you write about your child into a calm,
captioned animation they can follow. Your story is personal — often about a
child with special needs — so we treat it with care. Here is exactly what
happens to it.

## What we ask you for

- **Your story**, in your own words.
- **Your child's first name** (and a nickname, if you use one) — only so the
  video can say it. We don't need their surname, birthday, address, school, or
  any medical or diagnostic details. Please leave those out.

That's all. If your story happens to include something we didn't need, we'll
gently point it out so you can remove it — we never store the flagged text.

## What we do with it

- Your story is used **only** to make *your* family's animation. Never for
  advertising, never sold or shared for anyone else's purposes.
- Your child's name is removed before the story is sent to the AI that helps
  write the scenes, and added back only when the video is made on our side. The
  saved script holds a placeholder, not the name.
- **We never use your story to train or improve any AI.** We use an AI provider
  configured so your text is not retained by them and not used for training.

## A person checks it before your child sees it

Every animation is a **draft** until a human reviews it. Nothing is shown to your
child automatically — a person looks at the video and the captions first, and
only then is it approved.

## How long we keep it, and deleting it

- We keep your story only as long as needed to make and deliver the animation,
  and then delete it — within **30 days** — unless you choose to save it.
- You can **delete** your story, its script, and its animation at any time. When
  you do, we remove all of it, including copies in our backups on the next cycle.

## Questions or requests

You are in control of your child's data. To ask a question, or to delete what
you've shared, contact us at **wegofwd2020@gmail.com**.
